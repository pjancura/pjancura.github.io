About Me

Hi, I’m Paul. I am a software developer with a strong foundation in adaptability, leadership, and aligning team goals. Throughout my career, I’ve consistently leveraged software to improve workflows and solve problems. As a music and technology teacher, I demonstrated adaptability by using Google products to create engaging instructional aids tailored to diverse learning needs. While working as a store manager in a new country, I showcased leadership by mentoring team members and taught myself to code database queries, streamlining inventorying store products and aligning team efforts. Upon returning to the U.S., I embraced a growth mindset by completing the Cleveland Codes program at Cuyahoga Community College gaining several programming certifications. I was also invited back by the program leads to contribute to the rewrite of the Help Is Here app, a resource focused on mental health and suicide prevention awareness. I’m excited to bring my creativity, curiosity, and technical skills to a role where I can build innovative tools that enhance people’s lives and foster collaboration within teams.
